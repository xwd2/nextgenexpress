IFTA Quarterly Tax Filings

We handle all aspects of your International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) quarterly reporting and filing. From tracking mileage and fuel consumption by jurisdiction to calculating taxes owed or credits due, we manage the entire process so you stay compliant every quarter.

  • Complete quarterly IFTA tax preparation
  • Accurate calculations for all jurisdictions
  • Detailed mileage and fuel tracking by state/province
  • Timely filing to meet quarterly deadlines
  • Compliance with IFTA requirements
  • Direct submission to your base jurisdiction

Perfect for: Interstate carriers who need reliable quarterly IFTA tax filing without the stress of tracking fuel purchases and mileage across multiple jurisdictions.

What's Included

Multi-Jurisdiction Expertise

We handle IFTA calculations for all U.S. states and Canadian provinces where you operate. Each jurisdiction has specific requirements - we know them all.

Quarterly Preparation

From gathering your fuel purchase data and trip logs to calculating tax liability or credits for each quarter, we handle every step of the preparation process.

Filing & Submissions

We submit all IFTA returns to your base jurisdiction on your behalf, ensuring they're filed correctly and on time to avoid penalties.

Record Keeping

We maintain detailed records of all your IFTA filings, fuel purchases, and mileage data to ensure you're audit-ready at all times.
